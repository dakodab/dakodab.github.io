About

Dakota Bragato is a freelance journalist and data visualization designer based in London. She works at the intersection of public health and cultural reporting, with a particular focus in reproductive justice. Her experience includes reporting for Raven News, and editorial work for Street Sense Media. Before moving into journalism, she spent several years in perinatal and early childhood health policy research, and has also worked in nonprofit communications, community organizing, and arts and tech education. She holds a Master’s in Digital Journalism from Goldsmiths, University of London, and a Bachelor’s in American Studies from Cornell University.
